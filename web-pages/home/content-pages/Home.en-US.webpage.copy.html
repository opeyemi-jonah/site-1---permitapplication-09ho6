NRC New Reactor Licensing Process

Understanding the comprehensive review process for new nuclear reactor licensing

1
Environmental Review

Required by the National Environmental Policy Act, Endangered Species Act, and the National Historic Preservation Act. This comprehensive review assesses the environmental impact of the proposed nuclear facility.

2
Safety Review

Required by the Atomic Energy Act of 1954, as Amended. This thorough safety evaluation ensures the proposed reactor design meets all safety requirements and regulatory standards.

3
Hearings

Required by the Atomic Energy Act of 1954 as Amended. Public hearings provide an opportunity for stakeholder input and ensure transparency in the licensing decision process.
